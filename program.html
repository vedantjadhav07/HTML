DEPARTMENT OF ELECTRONICS & TELECOMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

 Vision “Transforming Students into skilled technicians in the field of Electronics and
Telecommunication with changing needs of the Industry and Society”
Mission

M1: Providing state of the art laboratories for enhancing technical skill.
M2: Improving interaction with Industry.
M3: Developing soft skills by organizing and participating in social and extra-curricular Events.
Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

PEO-1: Provide solutions to related problems by applying fundamental knowledge of Mathematics, Science and Electronics and
Telecommunication Engineering.
PEO-2: Function individually or as a team in professional and ethical manner.
PEO-3: Adapt new technologies for continues improvement.
Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

PSO-1: Apply engineering skills for troubleshooting and maintenance of electronic and telecommunication equipment.
PSO-2: Use EDA tools and Software to develop simple electronics and telecommunication related circuits
